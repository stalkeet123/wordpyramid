How to Play

Instead of matching suits or numbers, you match Word Associations!

  • Select two cards from the same category (e.g., "Apple" & "Banana").
  • Clear the board to reveal cards underneath.
  • Strategically uncover the pyramid and draw pile.
  • 100% Solvable: Every level has a path to victory!
